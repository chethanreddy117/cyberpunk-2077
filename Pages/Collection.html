Guns in Cyberpunk 2077: Arm Yourself for Night City

In Cyberpunk 2077, guns are vital for surviving Night City’s dystopian streets. Players can wield futuristic firearms tailored to various playstyles, including Power weapons for ricocheting shots, Tech weapons for armor-piercing charges, and Smart weapons with guided projectiles. From pistols to sniper rifles, the game offers a range of weapons from common to legendary, with customizable mods and iconic designs. These high-tech arsenals are essential for dominating Night City’s chaos, whether you’re stealthy or aggressive.
