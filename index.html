Welcome To The Dark Future

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action-packed role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red and launched on December 10, 2020. The game takes you to Night City, a massive, futuristic city that's full of neon lights, advanced tech, powerful corporations, and a lot of chaos. You play as V, a mercenary whose look, background, and playstyle are completely up to you. The story gets intense when a chip in V’s head turns out to have the digital personality of Johnny Silverhand, a rebellious rocker played by Keanu Reeves. The dynamic between V and Johnny drives a big part of the story.

STORY

Take on the job of a lifetime, to steal a one of a kind implant that is the key to immortality, and build your legend in the vast open world of Night City, where the choices you make shape the story and the people around you. Take on a variety of gigs to go from up and coming mercenary to legendary cyberpunk, all while uncovering the mysteries of the priceless implant everyone is dying to get their hands on.
